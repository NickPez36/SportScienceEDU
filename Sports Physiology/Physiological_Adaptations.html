Targeted Adaptations

Zones T2 to T4 establish the foundation of peripheral efficiency and central cardiovascular capacity.

  • +++ Mito Biogenesis (AMPK): Driven by high volume in T2/T3.
  • +++ Fat Oxidation: Maximized in T2/T3 domains.
  • +++ Lactate Clearance: Trained heavily in T4 (Threshold).
